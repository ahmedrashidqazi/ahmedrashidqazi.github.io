01. About Me

Email Upwork Google Scholar

I work at the intersection of neuroscience and AI, with publications at top venues including Neuron (Cell Press), CVPR, NeurIPS, and AAAI. My research focuses on understanding how biological vision systems can inform machine learning, and how AI methods can advance neuroscience.

Beyond research, I build production systems — AI platforms and tools that solve practical problems. Whether it's training models on neural data, building multimodal AI for healthcare, or architecting backend infrastructure, I aim to create work that's both rigorous and useful.

I graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science from LUMS and attended Stanford University as a Pre-Collegiate Scholar.

About

I work at the intersection of neuroscience and AI, with publications at top journals and conferences. My research focuses on understanding how biological vision systems can inform machine learning, and how AI methods can advance neuroscience. I also build production systems — AI platforms and tools that solve practical problems. Whether it's training models on neural data or architecting backend infrastructure, I aim to create work that's both rigorous and useful.

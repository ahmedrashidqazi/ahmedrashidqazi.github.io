About

I am someone who enjoys exploring the space where research meets real-world impact. I love building things that actually get used, systems that solve problems and create value rather than just demonstrate ideas. My work often sits at the intersection of AI, healthcare, and life sciences, where curiosity meets purpose. Whether it is designing a model, refining a pipeline, or finding cleaner ways to connect research with production, I find the most joy in creating tools that make science a little faster, smarter, and more useful for people.

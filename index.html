01 / About

Hands-on engineering with research depth.

The emphasis here is practical execution: training, infrastructure, deployment, and enough research fluency to build better systems.

I'm a hands-on AI engineer who builds and ships production systems. From training and fine-tuning models to designing scalable ML pipelines and serving infrastructure, I focus on turning ideas into deployed products that work at scale.
